### Investigation Process

Step 1: Assigning the alert.
Step 2: Performing triage by identifying timestamps, user status, and determining malicious elements.
Step 3: Reviewing sender reputation and other recipents.
Step 4: Identifying proxy, DNS logs and lateral movement of unusual activity and connections.
Step 5: Drafting a report.
